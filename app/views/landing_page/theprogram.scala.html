What you should expect

An intense 8-week journey that takes you from being non-technical to working on your project at every part of the stack: frontend, backend, database and server, alongside several other highly motivated fullstack-founders-in-training. After the program is over, you'll be part of a community of alumni who are all continuing to grow their skills and helping each other in the process.
